About

Grounding technical climate work in Tribal stewardship priorities.

My work centers on making complex forest carbon and stewardship systems more useful, legible, and actionable for Tribal Nations and partners. That includes improved forest management strategy, technical advising, public speaking, and partnership building across nonprofit, academic, and private sector settings.
