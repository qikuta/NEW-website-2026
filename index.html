Introduction

Tribal lands, climate finance, and generational stewardship.

I work at the intersection of Tribal land stewardship, forest carbon, GIS, and Indigenous-centered climate strategy. This site highlights my forestry work, featured media, speaking engagements, research, and updates in a more navigable multi page format.

I currently represent the Indian Land Tenure Foundation (ILTF) - National Indian Carbon Coalition (NICC) as an Improved Forest Management Project Maanger.

My previous work experience includes serving with the USDA Forest Service on the Umatilla and Nantahala National Forests, Americorps, and the Federal Reserve Bank of Minneapolis.
